Visual Hierarchy

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

This site exemplifies the principle of visual hierarchy; it immediately draws your attention to the relevant content of the page, by placing them at the top of the page. Additionally, it clearly highlights a specific element by enlarging it and placing it above all the others. The enlarged element immediately catches the users attention, and the proceding elements follow the natural flow of the users browsing.

White Space and Clean Design

Codecademy

Codecademy.com

I feel that this site has efficient use of white space in with their content. Each element has sufficient white space to avoid touching other elements, and adds a nice, clean look to the page. Withing the elements of the page; they make good use of white space, with some space between bold header words and smaller paragraphs, and space around the words of certain elements, making the content easily readable while adding to the look of the page.

Repetition

Cookies and Cream Doodles

Cookies&Cream.com

I think this site exemplifies the principle of repetition; it uses the same color scheme throughout the entire site, giving the site a unified and consistent look. It also applies the same formatting on elements of a similar nature, again using the same consistent look for the entire site. General formatting remains the same from page to page keeping the same unity across the entire site.
