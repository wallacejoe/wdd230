Great Winters

Mesa is a popular destination for those seeking a winter not filled with the troublesome, though beautiful, snow that permeates other locations in the United States.

Beautiful Mesa Temple

One well known landmark of Mesa is the Latter-day Saint temple. During the Christmas season, the temple grounds are lit up with many Christmas lights, and tell the story of the first Christmas. You can celebrate your easter with the Mesa easter pageant, a performance of Christs life, ministry, crucifixion, and resurrection.

Plentiful Lakes

Mesa has many lakes within easy traveling distance, while also having good whether for swimming for most of the year.

Camping and Offroading

There are many good locations for camping near Mesa. There are also great locations to have some fun with your trucks, bikes, and other offroad vehicles while you hit the dirt.

Mesa Arts Center

The Mesa Arts Center is a must visit destination for tourists. Watch live performances at the largest art center in Arizona.

The Mesa housing market is a sellers market. Houses are sold fast and at higher than average prices.

As of 2022, Mesa has an overall population of more than 500k people.
